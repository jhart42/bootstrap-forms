Artemisia Gentileschi

A Quiz for Art Lovers

Artemisia Gentileschi was an Italian painter during the 17th century. In an age where female artists were not easily accepted, Gentileschi is remembered for breaking into the profession and becoming one of the most influential painters of her generation. She was known for her ability to convincingly depict the female figure in all stages of undress, in addition to her skill and talent regarding color in overall composition and in building depth.
